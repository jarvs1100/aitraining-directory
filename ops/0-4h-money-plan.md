# 0–4 Hour Revenue-First Execution Plan (AITraining.Directory)

Goal: produce cash opportunities fast, not just traffic.

## Hour 0–1: Offer + Checkout + Intake

## Offer stack (launch)
1. **Featured Listing (14 days)** — $49
2. **Category Top Spot (30 days)** — $149
3. **Done-for-you Listing Optimization** — $199 one-time

## Fast checkout setup
- Stripe payment links (3 links, one per offer)
- Add links to `/submit/` page and email templates

## Intake form fields (required)
- Tool name
- URL
- Category
- 1-line value proposition
- 3 key features
- Pricing model
- Contact email
- Purchased package (dropdown)

Success metric (hour 1): payments can be accepted immediately.

---

## Hour 1–2: Sales-ready pages + trust

Pages to ship now:
- `/submit/` (already live) → add paid package block + buy buttons
- `/advertise/` new page with package comparison + FAQ
- `/for-founders/` short page: "Get discovered by L&D buyers"

Trust assets:
- "Published within 48h" guarantee
- "If not published, full refund"
- "No fake traffic claims"

Success metric (hour 2): clear conversion path from visit → payment → intake.

---

## Hour 2–3: Outbound list + direct selling

Target buyer profile:
- AI tool founders in training, onboarding, SOP, compliance, LMS

Lead sources:
- Product Hunt newest AI launches
- There’s An AI For That new tools
- LinkedIn founders (AI/L&D niche)

Build first list: 100 prospects
Columns:
- tool
- founder/contact
- email/linkedin
- category fit
- angle
- status

Success metric (hour 3): 100 qualified leads ready.

---

## Hour 3–4: Outreach blast + follow-up engine

Send 40–60 personalized first touches:
- 20 email
- 20–40 LinkedIn/Twitter DM

Follow-up cadence:
- D+2: social proof + urgency
- D+5: final reminder with lower entry offer ($49)

KPIs for first 72h:
- 40+ outbound messages/day
- 10% reply rate
- 3–8 paid placements

---

## Pricing evolution after first 10 sales
- Featured listing: $49 → $79
- Category top spot: $149 → $249
- DFY optimization: keep $199

## Non-negotiables
- Keep niche tight (AI training/L&D)
- Don’t expand categories until paid demand is proven
- Revenue before scale
