# Traffic Acquisition SOP (AI Training Directory)

## Goal
Generate qualified L&D/operator traffic from organic search + distribution loops, then route users into solution pages and partner actions.

## Weekly Cadence

### Monday — SERP + Intent Mapping (60 min)
1. Pull top 20 landing pages by impressions/clicks from GSC.
2. Classify each by intent: **comparison**, **use-case**, **role**, **industry**, **localized**.
3. Identify 5 pages with high impressions + low CTR and queue title/meta rewrites.

### Tuesday — Content Depth Sprint (90 min)
1. Update 2 solution pages with:
   - practical checklist
   - rollout plan
   - internal links to compare/category/tool pages
2. Add FAQ with implementation-focused questions.
3. Ensure each page has clear CTA path to `/advertise/` or `/submit/`.

### Wednesday — Internal Linking Pass (45 min)
1. Add 3–5 contextual internal links from top pages into under-linked pages.
2. Ensure every new page links to:
   - `/solutions/`
   - `/compare/`
   - at least one `/tool/{slug}/` page
3. Validate no orphan pages in the latest sitemap.

### Thursday — Distribution + Partnerships (60 min)
1. Share one tactical page in:
   - founder/operator communities
   - L&D newsletters (manual outreach)
   - partner circles relevant to AI training workflows
2. Track placements and responses in outreach tracker.
3. Offer co-marketing package for founding partners.

### Friday — KPI Review + Next Week Queue (45 min)
1. Update `ops/daily-kpi-tracker.csv`.
2. Score weekly winners by:
   - clicks gained
   - ranking improvements
   - conversion events (partner/submit actions)
3. Prioritize next week backlog by **impact x effort**.

## Page-Level QA Checklist (Before Publish)
- [ ] Title and meta aligned with search intent.
- [ ] H1 + intro match user problem, not generic AI copy.
- [ ] At least one checklist and one decision-support section.
- [ ] Internal links to relevant compare/solution/tool pages.
- [ ] Canonical and hreflang are set correctly.
- [ ] FAQ schema reflects real buyer questions.

## Success Targets (90-day)
- +30% organic clicks on solution and compare pages
- 2x pages ranking top-20 for core commercial-intent terms
- 10+ partner leads from `/advertise/`
- Consistent growth of internal-link-assisted pages
