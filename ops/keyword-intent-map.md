# Phase 1 — Keyword → Intent Map (Evidence-backed)

Date: 2026-02-17 (UTC)  
Method: Brave SERP sampling across EN + PL/CZ/SK/HU/DE/ES queries; intent classification from observed ranking-page format (listicles, comparisons, solution/legal pages).

## Intent model used
- **Commercial investigation (high):** “best tools”, “alternatives”, “vs”
- **Operational solution (high):** workflow-specific queries (onboarding, compliance, multilingual, AI literacy)
- **Informational (medium):** general “what is / guide” patterns

## Cross-locale pattern takeaways
1. **“Best X tools” list format dominates** in every locale tested.
2. **“Alternatives” and “vs” pages are persistent bottom-funnel SERP formats** in EN and should be expanded.
3. **Operational legal/compliance demand exists (EU AI Act AI literacy)** and maps to high-trust solution pages.
4. **Most localized SERPs are generic educational listicles** (not L&D-ops-specific), creating a positioning gap for workflow-first pages.

## Immediate keyword targets selected
- Primary EN: **best ai tools for training and development** (high-confidence demand shape: recurring top-list SERPs)
- Secondary EN: **[tool] alternatives**, **[tool] vs [tool]**
- Strategic EU solution: **EU AI Act AI literacy training**
- Localization expansion targets: PL/DE/ES first, then CZ/SK/HU
